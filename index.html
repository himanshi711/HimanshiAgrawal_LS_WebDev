Game Overview

Smash Karts is mayhem! You go around in go-karts, but with a wild twistâ€”you're armed with all sorts of crazy weapons. You and your friends zoom through chaotic arenas, picking up power-ups like rockets, grenades, and machine guns to take each other out. It's all about outlasting your opponents and causing as much mayhem as possible.

Objective: The main goal is to destroy as many opponents as possible while avoiding being destroyed yourself.

Match Duration: Matches typically last a few minutes. The player with the most points at the end of the match wins.

Respawn: If your kart is destroyed, you will respawn after a short delay, allowing you to rejoin the action.

Game Highlights

  • Wild Weapons: Arm yourself with rockets, grenades, and machine guns.
  • Trippy Controls: The controls are hard to get right, but when you do. You can smash you opponents!
  • Chaotic Battles: Outlast your friends in action-packed mayhem.

How to Get Started

This is a mobile game, available on both android and Iphone. Go to your respective app store and search for "Smash Karts" and install the game. You are ready to go! Smash your friends away!


Tips: Try to get a hold of your controls, they are essential in the game. Sometimes the weapon that you get isn't the best one so swallow your pride and use it quickly to get a better one
